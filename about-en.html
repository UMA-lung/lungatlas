About this site

Lung cancer is hard. Honestly, really hard.

This is where I keep and organize my notes, with AI helping along the way. I do my best to read the original studies, but this site will almost certainly have mistakes.

For now, this is still more of a personal study notebook than a formal resource. Corrections are welcome. Since there's no contact form, they may need to be very loud.

I'd be very happy if this site helps even one person study lung cancer a little more easily.

I have no COI, which is another way of saying I'm still pretty early in my career.

⚠ Disclaimer
The content on this site is intended for educational and informational purposes only, and is not intended to guide individual clinical decisions. Clinical judgment should always be based on the latest guidelines, primary literature, and the treating physician's expertise. While every effort is made to keep content accurate and up to date, please refer directly to original publications for final clinical decision-making.

All content is free and open access.
